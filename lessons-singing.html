Singing Lessons

Private voice lessons for every age and ability — structured, encouraging and a lot of fun.

Jennifer makes you want to take lessons. She's fun, encouraging, and knows what she's talking about — which is so refreshing. My time with her as both a vocal and acting coach was so valuable, and helped me get into school to pursue a degree in Music Theater. I couldn't have done it without her guidance. — Michael B., student at University of Buffalo

I have openings for students who wish to take private voice lessons. All ages are welcome! If you have always wanted to sing but believe that you cannot carry a tune in a bucket, I would love to help you disprove that theory. And if you were blessed with a great ear and the vocal chops to match, I'd very much enjoy helping you become the strongest, healthiest singer possible — so you can enjoy your talent for years to come.

Lessons are structured but a lot of fun! Homework will be assigned and expected to be worked on in order to get the most out of the next session. Most lessons are 60 minutes; for children ages 6–12 and for adults on a tight budget or schedule, 30-minute lessons are available. Children can move up to 60-minute lessons based on focus, vocal maturity, and what would be most fun for them.

I teach on weekends as well as weekdays, with a strict 24-hour cancellation policy that applies to me (the teacher) too. :) If the lesson is for a child, the parent is welcome to have a cup of tea at my kitchen table while reading a book, or head out to run a few errands.

— Jennifer
