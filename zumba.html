Parent & Me Zumba

(Or grandparent, aunt, uncle, babysitter, or…)

  • When Wednesdays & Fridays at 9:30am
  • Where St. John's Gymnasium, 502 W Chestnut Street, Rome, NY
  • Fee $40 per session (8 classes) or $8 drop-in

This class runs based on interest. If you'd like it on the schedule, just contact Jennifer — she's more than willing to add it.

What is Zumba?

When participants see a Zumba class in action, they can't wait to give it a try. Classes feature exotic rhythms set to high-energy Latin and international beats. Before participants know it, they're getting fit and their energy levels are soaring. It's easy to do, effective and totally exhilarating — often building a deep-rooted community among returning students.

This class is for you if…

  • You're worried you “won't get the moves.”
  • You're sure you “won't get a real workout.”
  • You have not two, but three left feet.
