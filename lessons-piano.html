Piano Lessons

One-on-one or with a friend — for children and adults, beginner to intermediate.

Jennifer brings a wonderful attitude and energy to everything she does. Her light-hearted approach makes learning fun and encourages people to keep on learning. — Larry B., retired State worker

I am accepting new piano students, children and adults. If you have always wanted to learn to read music and begin to play your favorite hymns or radio tunes, this is where you want to start!

I wholeheartedly enjoy teaching one-on-one, but many enjoy taking lessons with a friend — two friends, parent-child, grandparent-grandchild. It's a fun way to learn! My studio has two electric pianos with 88 weighted keys (one Yamaha, one Roland) and headphones for each student, so part of the lesson is one-on-one and part is learning together.

I've been playing piano for over 20 years and teaching for 15. My forte is teaching the beginner student and taking them to an intermediate level. Beginning lessons use Bastien Piano for Adults, Level 1 (green cover, about $16); younger students also work from individual worksheets and songs. Lessons are structured but a lot of fun, with homework assigned between sessions.

I teach weekends and weekdays, with a strict 24-hour cancellation policy that applies to me too. :)
