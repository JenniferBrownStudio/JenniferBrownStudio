I have openings for students who wish to take private voice lessons. All ages are welcome! If you have always wanted to 'sing' but believe that you cannot carry a tune in a bucket - I would LOVE to help you disprove that theory. Or, if you were blessed with a great ear and vocal chops to match, I'd very much enjoy helping you become the strongest and healthiest singer possible, so that you can enjoy your talent for years and years to come.

Lessons/classes will be structured but a lot of fun! Homework will be assigned and expected to be worked on in order to get the most out of the next session. Most lessons are 60 minutes. However, for children ages 6-12 and/or for adults on a tight budget or time frame, 30 minute lessons are available. Children can move to 60 min lessons based on focus, vocal maturity and what they think would be most fun for them.

I do teach on weekends as well as weekdays. However, I have a strict 24 hour cancellation policy. It applies to me (the teacher) as well. :) Sometimes life throws us curve balls, but we need to be respectful of each other's #1 resource, time. During the lesson, if the lesson is for a child, the parent is more than welcome to have a cup of tea at my kitchen table while reading a book, or head out and run a few errands.

Rates for a 1 hour lesson are:

  • 1st lesson = $30
  • One lesson at a time = $40
  • 5 Lessons = $175 (will expire 6 weeks from date of purchase)
  • 10 Lessons = $300 (will expire 12 weeks from date of purchase)

Rates for a 30 min lesson are:

  • 1st lesson = $18
  • One lesson at a time = $25
  • 5 Lessons = $110 (will expire 6 weeks from date of purchase)
  • 10 Lessons = $200 (will expire 12 weeks from date of purchase)

If at your home, a reasonable additional fee will be added to each lesson. Also, please try to keep pets and other children away from where the lessons will take place. I want the student to get the most out of their lesson time.

If you have any questions, please don't hesitate to contact me!
-Jennifer Bernstone
