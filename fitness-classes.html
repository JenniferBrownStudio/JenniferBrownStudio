Fitness · Classes

Pilates, Indoor Walking & Weights

Approachable group classes for strength, posture and steady weight loss — very fun, very social.

Pilates

  • When Mon & Wed, 4:30–5:00pm
  • Where St. John's Gymnasium, Rome, NY
  • Fee $40 / session or $5 drop-in

Strengthen and stretch the abdomen and torso using only the body. Amazing results for anyone with a bad back, flabby tummy or poor posture. Please bring a mat.

Indoor Walking

  • When Mon & Wed, 5:00pm
  • Where St. John's Gymnasium, Rome, NY
  • Fee $80 / session or $8 drop-in

Geared for anyone wanting to drop 20+ lbs. If exercise is not your forte, this is definitely for you — very fun and very social! As long as you can walk in place, you can do this class.

Weight Training

  • When Tue & Thu, last 15 min of Zumba
  • Where St. John's Gym, 502 W Chestnut St, Rome
  • Bring 3- or 5-lb dumbbells

Built into the final stretch of each Zumba class. Filled water bottles work too. Unsure what to bring? Just ask — don't hesitate to contact Jennifer.

Class times and sessions run year-round in rotating 8-week blocks. Contact Jennifer for the current schedule and to reserve your spot.
