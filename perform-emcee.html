Emcee / Host

Host of your event

With decades on stage and a natural ease with a live audience, Jennifer is a confident, charismatic host for weddings, bar & bat mitzvahs, holiday and corporate parties, and community celebrations — keeping the energy up, the program moving and the room engaged. Since 2021 she has emceed 73+ events across Central New York in partnership with The Great Music Company.
